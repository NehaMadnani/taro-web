Taro - Privacy Policy

Introduction

Welcome to Taro, a Chrome extension designed to enhance your browsing experience. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use Taro.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: We do not collect any personal information such as your name, email address, or contact details.
  • Usage Data: We collect data on how you interact with Taro, including the features you use and the duration of your use.
  • Extension Performance Data: Information related to the performance of the extension, such as error reports and diagnostic data.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected to:

  • Improve the functionality and performance of Taro.
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance user experience.
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues.

Data Security

We take data security seriously and implement appropriate measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Third-Party Services

Taro does not share your information with third-party services. We do not integrate with any external services that collect or process your data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
